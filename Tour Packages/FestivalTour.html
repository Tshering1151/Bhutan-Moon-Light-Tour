Festival & Celebration Tour

Experience Bhutan’s Living Culture and Colorful Festivals

Join the vibrant rhythm of Bhutan’s most spectacular festivals through our **Festival & Celebration Tour**. This journey brings you face-to-face with centuries-old traditions, sacred mask dances, and the spiritual joy that fills the air during the country’s grand Tshechus and local fairs.

Witness locals in their finest attire, spinning prayer wheels, sharing laughter, and rejoicing in community harmony. Each festival is a celebration of life, faith, and happiness — a chance to see Bhutan’s cultural soul in full bloom.

🎊 Tour Highlights

  • Attend the grand Paro or Thimphu Tshechu featuring sacred mask dances
  • Witness local rituals, music, and folk performances
  • Explore famous dzongs and monasteries in festival settings
  • Experience authentic Bhutanese cuisine and traditional dress (Gho & Kira)
  • Capture the colorful celebrations with guided photography opportunities

📅 Duration

7 Days / 6 Nights

💰 Tour Price

Starting from $1,950 USD per person (includes accommodation, meals, guide & festival passes)
